Who we are?

Udgama started with a vision to build a group of techies passionate about coming up with new and better ways to do things. A bunch which is always in learning and development mode, likes sharing knowledge and encouraging people to be innovative. We strive to be early adopters of better tools, technologies, and processes and aim to contribute back to the tech community as well.

What we do ?

For now, we focus on building products and offering Product Development Services. We work with great backend development teams to bring their frontends to life and build backends with serverless technologies exposing GraphQL and REST based APIs. Some of the tools of our trade are TypeScript, ReactJS, React-Native, AWS, Serverless.
